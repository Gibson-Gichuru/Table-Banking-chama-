About

A simple implementation of the round circle banking system.

The application offers member(user) management system, C2B( member to circle fund deposits), B2C(circle to member fund disbasment) and member assitance bot

Tools Used

  • Python (Flask)
  • Postgress (Production Database)
  • Daraja Api( Payment gateway:-both C2B and B2C)
  • Redis-workers (Handling scheduled fund disbusment)
  • Linode (Production hosting service)
  • Telegram API (User Bot Assistances)
